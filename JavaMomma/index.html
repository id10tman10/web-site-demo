Why are we different?

JavaMomma is more than a coffee shop; it’s an extension of the kitchen table. We know that as women, mothers, and friends, our days are full. We’ve created this space specifically for you to pause. Whether you are joining a small group study or meeting a mentor for a latte, our goal is that you leave our doors feeling more fueled by the Word than by the caffeine.
