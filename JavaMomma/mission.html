Our Mission

Providing a sacred space where women can gather, grow in the Word, and find refreshment for both body and soul.

Born from a Passion for Covenant Community and Great Coffee

We believe that life is best lived in community, and that the most meaningful conversations happen over a shared cup. At JavaMomma, we aim to facilitate an environment where the 'daily grind' meets the eternal Truth. Our mission is to support the local church by providing a neutral, warm ground for discipleship, where women are encouraged to dig deep into Scripture and find the spiritual rest that only Christ provides.
